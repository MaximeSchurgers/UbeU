Frequently Asked Questions 

Any questions you have hopefully will be answered here.

Where can I buy the beer?

You can find UBeU beer in most common supermarkets.

What is the beer made of?

Beer is made out of water, malt, hop and yeast.

Where can I contact the company?

You can find our contact information and social media at the bottom of the website

How is UBeU sustainable ?

UBeU is sustainable because we use recycled ingredients in our product. Furthermore, the bottles of our beer are also made of recycled glass. The beer caps of our brand we recycle to pins. Click on the question to be directed to the right section for more information. 

How is this brand pro equality?

UBeU brand is pro equality, we are standing for the rights for both women and men. We wanted to make a beer brand that is focusing on females as well as males. This beer is made for everyone, striving for equal rights. UBeU sees every person as an individual and wants to make everyone feel included.

What does UBeU stand for?

Our brand name stands for You Be United.

What are UBeU's values?

The values of UBeU can be summarized by C.R.U.S.H. This stands for, Community, Refreshing, Unity, Sustainable and Happiness.

Where can I find who the owners of UBeU Brand are?

You can find who the owners are and gather some background information too.

What is the UBeU brew process?

The process of how our beer is brewed and more information.

How are you supposed to drink UBeU beer?

You should drink this beer with your friends together , and drinking it cold is the best way to drink it.
