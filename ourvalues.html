Our Values - C.R.U.S.H.

UBeU has its own values to focus on, to reach our purposes. Our values can be summarized as C.R.U.S.H.

Community

We believe in a strong sense of community and bringing people together. It takes a lot of people to make a radical change. It cannot only be one person.

Refreshing

Our beer is fresh and tastes as a new world opens up. We want our beer to be breathtaking. UBeU beer is refreshing, a drink to reset yourself with your friends.

Unity

We want to unite people again. We want everyone to feel equal and know they have the same rights as every other individual. Instead of being against each other, let’s be unity with each other.

Sustainable

We believe we should stop the depletion of natural resources and instead recycle and upcycle the materials we have right now. UBeU wants a future and the world needs to change to keep on living.
UBeU is made out of recycled products and ingredients. he glass of our product is also made out of recycled glass. Everyone that drinks UBeU is creating our future.

Happiness

UBeU is made to be drunk with our friends. UBeU is made to make you feel happy and joyful. We believe that UBeU beer can bring us all together, and make everyone smile. We all need to be happy again.
