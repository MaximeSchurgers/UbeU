Made in The Netherlands 

Beer in the Netherlands 

UBeU is made in the Netherlands. The country that knows many beers. UBeU is one of the beers that comes from our small country. The beer industry has grown a lot in the last few years in the Netherlands. At the moment there are 850 breweries here, additionally all the beer that is being consumed in the Netherlands, 85% of that is also being brewed here. Many new companies have entered the market, and different kinds of beer have been introduced as well. For example, fruit beer, white beer, non-alcoholic beer and bock beer. There are many options for everyone, nowadays every person likes beer. Special beer has grown 6.7% from 2020 to 2021. People want to enjoy beer more and not only when they are going out but also when they are enjoying their dinner or when hanging out with friends. .
