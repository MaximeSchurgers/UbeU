The Brew Process  

How we are sustainable

We use sustainable resources and leave behind less waste. We use an ecoconsious approach, meaning we keep the future in mind and tread carefully with every step. The beer bottles are made from special glass that uses less resources to produce. We upcycle as much water as we can, so there is no depletion of our natural resources. During rainy season we collect as much water as possible and also use that in our brew process. We also recover carbon dioxide from the fermentation cycle, so the carbon dioxide doesn't end up in the atmosphere and further amplifies the greenhouse effect. Currently we are striving to achieve our carbon-neutral certification
