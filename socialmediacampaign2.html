social media campaign  

follow us on @instagram!    

context of campaign Our campaign is about creating brand awareness, we want to spread a positive message and get our target audience interested in our brand and product. We created an instagram page with lots of posts and stories. Our main goal is that our audience knows about the existence of our brand and product and that they feel a positive feeling towards our beer and vision. We chose instagram and facebook, because instagram is the most used app for people in our age group. We decided to use facebook because it is a more professional platform. learning point Our learning point was to look into how the marketing via instagram works, to create an instagram page that is attractive to our audience. We were very excited and started t create different sorts of posts, but when we posted them we found them not really matching and it made the page look messy. therefore we deleted everything and started all over again, by creating a page that looks better and cleaner, we wanted color palette that made it looked like a whole. We learned how to create a reel and different sorts of posts, thinking about ways to create interaction. What we would do differently next time is looking more to other brands and look to their instagrampage. Most inspiration now came mostly from all kinds of beverage brands we researched and by getting our inspiration out of too many different companies, it created a messy instagrampage. When we started creating our own brand image the page looked more professional and cohesive. In addition, we learned how to make a facebook business page, which was very interesting and we learned a lot from it. future planning Up till now, we have created an Instagram page and a Facebook page.
