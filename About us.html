About us 

Learn more about the creators of The ubeu Beer Brand  

UBeU stands For U Be United, because only together we can make the change for a better future!  

Maxime Schurgers    

I am Maxime Schurgers and co-founder of UBeU brand . I am very excited for this product that we have created. We have put all our creative ideas and love into this product. It is important that we have created this brand because young people are the future generation to carry on this planet and I think it is part our job to fix the problems. With UBeU we created a fun and young brand to create a community but also at the same time a brand to help the environment and create a future.

Jazlyn Phillip 

My name is Jazlyn phillip, co-founder of UBeU brand. I'm orginally    from Aruba but moved to The Netherlands two years ago. The main idea behind our brand is to create a place where people can come together and help the environment at the same time. We believe that it is up to us to help the planet and save it from the damages we have already caused. Together with the other founders we believe we can make the difference that will save the planet, but we can't do it without your help.

Aniek Schrauwen 

My name is Aniek Schrauwen, co- founder of UBeU brand. UBeU    stands for U be United, it means that we are all connected and some things are uniting us. Beer is a product that people drink everywhere around the world. It is something that everyone knows and is popular everywhere on this globe. It unites us, because it is something we often drink together, it is associated with friends, family, good times. Our brand shares that idea. We want to contribute to a greener earth by making our beer sustainable. This way it is uniting AND helpful for the future.
