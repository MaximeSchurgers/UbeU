Aniek Schrauwen 

My name is Aniek Schrauwen, co- founder of the brand UbeU. U be U stands for U be United, it means that we are all connected and some things are uniting us. Beer is a product that people drink everywhere around the world. It is something that everyone knows and is popular everywhere on this globe. It unites us, because it is something we often drink together, it is associated with friends, family, good times. Our brand shares that idea. We want to contribute to a greener earth by making our beer sustainable. This way it is uniting AND helpful for the future.
